About Me

Welcome to my games portfolio!
My name is Madison and I am and young game maker with a Bachelor of Game Design and Development from Macquarie University my dream is to make games that show off naratives through many means if that be through classic platformers to rhythm games and more.

Projects

Disco Daimyo DX (School Project)

Role: Narrative Designer, Song Mapper

Disco Daimyo DX is a rhythm game thematically set in a highschool where you must bust-a-move against other students. From the classical movements of the orchestral band club to the soft indie beats of the poetry club, Can you master these songs and take down all the club members?

Issues (School Project)

Role: Creative Director, Asset Creator, Level Designer

Issues is a travesal and speedy platformor inspired by the "Animation Vs." series. The goal of the player is to escape the page of a comic book before the ink blots over the way out.

Virtual Reality Police Interrogation (School Project)

Role: Solo Developer, Solo Designer, Solo Writer

This experience puts the player in the position of a possible suspect of a crime. The police will question the player and dependent on the player's heart rate the environment will change as they are pushed into a stressful situation. This project was a exploration into immersion using virtual reality and heart rate can factor into people's choices in narrative games.

Project Paint Runner (In Early Development)

Role: Narrative Designer, Lead Writer, Mechanics Developer

Project Paint Runner is a momentum-based 3D platformer that takes place in the works of someone's entire life. The player is a paintbrush escaping the draining colour phenomenon happening to their world.

Issues (In Early Development)

Role: Lead Developer, Lead Designer, Narrative designer, Lead Writer

This version of Issues will be more true to my vision. Issues is a 2D Beat'em-up focused on the stories of superheroes. They will leap from panel to panel and page to page. The player will fight classic villains of the week and be absorbed in a comic book style story.
